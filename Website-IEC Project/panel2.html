MCC PANEL

MCC stands for Motor Control Center.It consists of feeder for motors and blowers.It is an assembly of one or more enclosed sections having a common power bus and principally conatining motor unit.

A motor control center can either be supplied ready for costumer to connect all field wiring or can be engineered assembly with internal command and interlocking wiring to central control terminal panel.

PCC stands for Power Control Center.They are most essential part of electrical system of an industry from where the power of industry is controlled.It's design is robust and ridigly constructed to suit a wide range of industries and buildings.Bus Bars/Internal Wiring uniform cross section and supported by SMC/DMC bases.
